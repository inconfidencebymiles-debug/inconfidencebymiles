The Miles Zone: 2 screens, billiards and atmosphere

The front of our venue is the open bar area: two screens positioned so you can see clearly from the counter, the tables or the terrace. While the match plays, the billiards table keeps going, alongside premium beers (Selecta, Brutus), long drinks and cocktails from €10.

This is where we come to watch a Champions League night on a Tuesday, an England or USA national match, or any big fixture. Warm atmosphere, adult crowd, no chanting hooligans.

In Confidence: book your own private box

For the matches that really matter — a Champions League final, a World Cup game, a key derby — we offer something different from any sports bar in Barcelona: the In Confidence room at the back of the venue, with its 2 dedicated screens, independent bar, signature cocktails from €10 and optional DJ.

  • Full In Confidence room — up to 60 people
  • Salvaje room (within In Confidence) — up to 30 people, ideal for more intimate groups
